Consumer

Target

Guys looking for a premium dip experience. They’re self-confident, uncomplicated, and know BS when they see, hear, and taste it. They like who they are, and like brands that represent them, not ones who try to define them.

Product

Ideal

A damn good tobacco experience for modern dippers, that is, guys wholike to mix it up but still enjoy the comfort and consistency of an EDD.

Emotional

Benefit

Everything Grizzly does — their dip, their packaging, hell, even their marketing — strengthens my own resolve: If you’re not living life on your own terms, you’re living someone else’s.

Consumer

Insight

These are guys who respect tradition, but aren’t mired in it. You might not call them trailblazers, but you sure as hell wouldn’t call them stagnant. They’ll try anything once – brand, flavor, form – because variety means adventure, and that means doing things on their own terms.

Brand

Ideal

Reason to

Believe

Consumer

Tension

These are guys who refuse to be told what to think, how to act, how to talk, who to be, and what they should and shouldn’t do with their free time. This is also the 21st century, where our world has become one that tries to tell us all of those things 24/7.
